KUMERICA

"Kumerica" is a term that emerged to describe a subculture and movement centered around the city of Kumasi in Ghana. It gained popularity through social media, particularly on platforms like Twitter and Instagram. The term is a fusion of "Kumasi," the capital city of the Ashanti Region in Ghana, and "America."

Kumerica is often associated with a unique blend of Ghanaian and American pop culture, fashion, and lifestyle. It has gained recognition for its vibrant music scene, fashion trends, and the creative expression of the youth in Kumasi.
